States of India

Andhra Pradesh

Andhra Pradesh is a state located in southeastern India. It is known for its rich cultural heritage, historic landmarks, and delicious food. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Charminar, the Tirumala Venkateswara Temple, and the Araku Valley.

Arunachal Pradesh

Arunachal Pradesh is a state located in northeastern India. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, including the Tawang Monastery, the Namdapha National Park, and the Sela Pass. The state is also famous for its unique culture and traditional handicrafts.

Assam

Assam is a state located in northeastern India. It is known for its tea plantations, wildlife sanctuaries, and historical landmarks such as the Kamakhya Temple and the Umananda Temple. The state is also famous for its silk and bamboo handicrafts.

Bihar

Bihar is a state located in eastern India. It is known for its rich history, ancient monuments, and diverse culture. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Mahabodhi Temple, the Nalanda University ruins, and the Bodhi Tree.

Chhattisgarh

Chhattisgarh is a state located in central India. It is known for its tribal culture, ancient temples, and natural beauty. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Chitrakoot Falls, the Jagdalpur Palace, and the Bhoramdeo Temple.

Goa

Goa is a state located on the western coast of India. It is known for its stunning beaches, vibrant nightlife, and Portuguese heritage. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Basilica of Bom Jesus, the Dudhsagar Falls, and the Fort Aguada.

Gujarat

Gujarat is a state located in western India. It is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and scenic beauty. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Gir Forest National Park, the Somnath Temple, and the Rann of Kutch.

Haryana

Haryana is a state located in northern India. It is known for its rich history, traditional culture, and agricultural prosperity. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Sultanpur Bird Sanctuary, the Surajkund Mela, and the Kurukshetra Panorama and Science Centre.

Himachal Pradesh

Himachal Pradesh is a state located in northern India. It is known for its stunning natural beauty, including the snow-capped peaks of the Himalayas, scenic hill stations, and lush forests. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Rohtang Pass, the Shimla Mall Road, and the Dharamshala Cricket Stadium.

Jharkhand

Jharkhand is a state located in eastern India. It is known for its rich mineral resources, tribal culture, and natural beauty. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Betla National Park, the Baidyanath Temple, and the Dassam Falls.

Karnataka

Karnataka is a state located in southern India. It is known for its rich history, cultural heritage, and diverse landscape. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Hampi ruins, the Mysore Palace, and the Jog Falls.

Kerala

Kerala is a state located in southern India. It is known for its natural beauty, diverse culture, and delicious cuisine. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the backwaters of Alleppey, the beaches of Kovalam, and the hill station of Munnar.

Madhya Pradesh

Madhya Pradesh is a state located in central India. It is known for its rich history, ancient monuments, and diverse wildlife. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Khajuraho temples, the Kanha National Park, and the Sanchi Stupa.

Maharashtra

Maharashtra is a state located in western India. It is known for its bustling cities, beautiful beaches, and rich cultural heritage. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Gateway of India, the Ajanta and Ellora Caves, and the beaches of Goa.

Manipur

Manipur is a state located in northeastern India. It is known for its natural beauty, rich cultural heritage, and traditional martial arts. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions including the Loktak Lake, the Keibul Lamjao National Park, and the Kangla Fort.

Meghalaya

Meghalaya is a state located in northeastern India. It is known for its scenic hills, waterfalls, and natural beauty. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Living Root Bridges, the Nohkalikai Falls, and the Mawlynnong village.

Mizoram

Mizoram is a state located in northeastern India. It is known for its beautiful hills, dense forests, and rich tribal culture. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Dampa Tiger Reserve, the Phawngpui Peak, and the Palak Lake.

Nagaland

Nagaland is a state located in northeastern India. It is known for its scenic beauty, rich cultural heritage, and traditional handicrafts. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Dzukou Valley, the Kohima War Cemetery, and the Hornbill Festival.

Odisha

Odisha is a state located in eastern India. It is known for its ancient temples, rich history, and diverse wildlife. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Sun Temple of Konark, the Chilika Lake, and the Simlipal National Park.

Punjab

Punjab is a state located in northern India. It is known for its rich agricultural heritage, historic monuments, and vibrant culture. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Golden Temple, the Jallianwala Bagh, and the Wagah Border.

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is a state located in western India. It is known for its vibrant culture, ancient forts, and palaces, and scenic desert landscape. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Amer Fort, the Jaipur City Palace, and the Thar Desert.

Sikkim

Sikkim is a state located in northeastern India. It is known for its scenic mountains, vibrant culture, and rich biodiversity. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Tsomgo Lake, the Nathula Pass, and the Rumtek Monastery.

Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is a state located in southern India. It is known for its ancient temples, rich culture, and beautiful beaches. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Meenakshi Amman Temple, the Marina Beach, and the Nilgiri Mountain Railway.

Telangana

Telangana is a state located in southern India. It is known for its rich history, vibrant culture, and IT industry. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Charminar, the Ramoji Film City, and the Golconda Fort.

Tripura

Tripura is a state located in northeastern India. It is known for its beautiful landscapes, rich culture, and traditional handicrafts. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Unakoti Hill, the Neermahal Palace, and the Sepahijala Wildlife Sanctuary.

Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh is a state located in northern India. It is known for its ancient temples, rich history, and diverse culture. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Taj Mahal, the Varanasi Ghats, and the Fatehpur Sikri.

Uttarakhand

Uttarakhand is a state located in northern India. It is known for its scenic mountains, beautiful lakes, and rich cultural heritage. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Valley of Flowers National Park, the Jim Corbett National Park, and the Kedarnath Temple.

West Bengal

West Bengal is a state located in eastern India. It is known for its rich history, cultural diversity, and delicious cuisine. The state is home to several famous tourist attractions, including the Victoria Memorial, the Sundarbans National Park, and the Darjeeling Himalayan Railway.
